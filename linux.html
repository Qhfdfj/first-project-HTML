Biography

Linux Torvalds is a Finnish-American software engineer who is the creator and principle developer of the Linux kernel,which is the kernel for a number of operating systems. He also created the version control system Git. Torvalds was born on December 28,1969 in Helsinki,Finland. He studies computer science at the University of Helsinki,and he developer the Linux kernel as a student project in 1991.Since then,the linux kernel has beccome one of the most widely used kernels in the world,powering everything from smartphon ACM SIGOPes to supercomputers.
